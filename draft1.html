A Truly Customised Experience

our app uses facial recognition to unlock and start the car, also make customised adjustments to carseat level, preferred music playlist, preferred air conditioning, etc.

Detect Oversteer and Understeer

Turning can be problematic if your steering is wrong, our app detects oversteer and understeer and alerts the user to perform corrective measures.

Efficient Route Calculation

Have many locations to visit? Just enter all the places you need to visit and our app calculates and guides you through the most effective route possible to visit all your destinations.

Obstacle detection

Tackling speed breakers and road obstacles like pot holes can be dangerous, the app detects any upcoming obstacles and alerts the passenger to slow down and take necessary precautions.
